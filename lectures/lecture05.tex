Terminology
\begin{itemize}
	\item $\Delta X$ is the \textbf{dynamic range}
	\item $q$ is the quantization resolution
\end{itemize}

The dynamic range, quantization resolution, and number of bits are related by 
\begin{equation*}
q = \frac{\Delta X}{2^{B}}
\end{equation*}
